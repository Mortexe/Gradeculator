What is Gradeculator?

Need a quick and easy way of calculating your average grade without spending time calculating it yourself?

Gradeculator has your back with a user-friendly and simple UI.

Gradeculator uses a simple mathematical algorithm to calculate the average of your module assessment, which comes in handy when you need a quick calculation without the need of doing all that yucky math.

Example

Let's say you have a module which consists of 2 different assignments throughout the year and their total weight is 80% (e.g Assessment A counts 50% and Assessment B counts 30%)

Mark Weight Reference
80 50% Long Essay
68 30% Essay Report
Average mark out of 80% = 60.4%

Now you have an average out of 80%. If you want the full 100% you can estimate the remaining 20% weight and calculate it and you'll have an approximate assessment average.
